From booking and branding to release strategy and touring, we handle every dimension of an artist's career so they can focus on what matters: the music.

Everything you need to know about working with HVX Music.

Send us a link to your music (SoundCloud, Spotify, or a private mix) along with a short bio to demo@hvxmusic.com. We review all submissions and respond within 2–3 weeks. We look for artists with a strong, distinct sound and a clear artistic vision — genre alone doesn't define who we work with.

We offer full artist management including booking & tour coordination, brand development, release strategy, social media direction, press & PR, sync & licensing opportunities, and long-term career planning. We're hands-on partners, not just booking agents.
